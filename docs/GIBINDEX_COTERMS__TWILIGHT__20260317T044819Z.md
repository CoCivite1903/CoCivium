# GIBindex Additions – CoTwilight & Load Management

## CoSat (Context Saturation)
Working context overload leading to degraded integration accuracy.

## CoThrDeg (Throughput Degradation)
Decline in response efficiency and synthesis quality under load.

## CoTwilight
Session phase where coherence transitions toward degradation.

## CoTwilightApproach
Protocol for detecting Twilight and triggering handoff or scope control.

## CoCarryMinimal
Handoff discipline: carry signal, not volume.

## CoSignalLoss
Failure to register/respond to recent critical input.

## CoLoadProfile
Measure of conceptual density and branching complexity.

## CoHandoffReady
State where fresh session yields higher return than continuation.

## CoDrift
Unintended deviation from original mandate.

## CoAnchorSet
Minimal truths required for continuity.

## CoResync
Explicit realignment step after drift or loss.

## CoPulse
Snapshot of system state at a given wave.

## CoExGap
Failure to externalize identified artifacts before session close.

## CoExGate
Mandatory checkpoint ensuring all CoEx artifacts are persisted before .done.
